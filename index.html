About Me

My name is Jonah Tuckman. I am currently in my third year at Vassar College majoring in Computer Science alongside a correlate in Statistical Mathematics.


I am passionate about uncovering the potential of machine learning and Artificial Intelligence practices in addressing and solving global issues and drawing this into collaboration with secure and protected Blockchain technology. Alongside the emergence of Blockchain, I see a world where personalized data belongs solely to the user, yet broad and anonymous data allows us to solve global issues such as cancer detection and treatments, renewable energy practices, autonomous automobiles, and many more.
