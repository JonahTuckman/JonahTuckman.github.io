About Me

My name is Jonah Tuckman and I am a multiple-time founder who has spent years building a strong acumen within the emerging business space.


I have a passion and a deep understanding of product management and growth and have worked diligently for years building out products in a variety of spaces. I learn incredibly quickly which has proven useful as I enter new spaces and take on new ideas and am disciplined in my working schedule and consistent growth.


In building companies myself, I have taken on responsibility quite above my years of experience which has resulted in immense learning of product design, management, growth, and more.


I have worked with reputable investment funds and individuals, some of the largest manufacturers in Asia, venture-backed startup executives and founders, fortune 500 executives, cross-functional teams, legal teams, marketers, and more which has propelled me to a deep experience in all areas of product management.


Personally, I am passionate about the ways that technology can utilize data to solve widespread problems that we are faced with daily.
